Why we built this

When I lost my dog, I wanted something more than a photo on my phone. I wanted something that captured who he was — not just what he looked like, but the way he made me feel. The goofy nickname. The way he'd steal socks. The morning routine we had that I didn't appreciate until it was gone.

I couldn't find anything that did that. So I built it.

Every tribute we make is personal. The poem is written from the details you share — your memories, your words, your love. Then we print it alongside their photo in a frame that belongs on your wall forever.
