Questions about dog memorial gifts

What makes this different from other dog memorial gifts?

Most dog memorials stamp a name on a stone or ornament. Ours is a personalized poem written from the specific memories you share – their quirks, their personality, the things only your family would know. Printed alongside their photo in a handcrafted frame.

Does the poem actually mention things specific to my dog?

Yes. The poem is built from the details you share. If your dog stole socks, greeted everyone at the door, or had a special spot on the couch, those details go into the poem. It reads like someone who knew them wrote it.

What if I only have a phone photo?

That works perfectly. We color-correct, upscale, and optimize every image for print. Even a casual phone photo of your dog comes out sharp and beautiful in the finished frame.

Can I send this as a gift to someone who just lost their dog?

Absolutely. Many of our orders are sympathy gifts. Even just a name and photo make a beautiful tribute. We guide you through it and can ship directly to the recipient.

Can I edit the poem before it's printed?

Yes. You see a full preview with the photo, poem, and frame style before anything goes to print. Edit any line, regenerate the poem, or choose from our curated library. You have complete control.

Do you make memorial gifts for other pets too?

Yes. We create personalized memorial tributes for cats, horses, rabbits, birds, and any companion animal. Visit our Pet Memorial Gifts page or our Cat Memorial Gifts page for more.
