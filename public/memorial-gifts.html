Questions you might have

How does this work?

You share memories about the person who passed – their personality, things they said, how they made people feel. We write a first-person letter from their perspective, as if they're speaking to the people they love. You approve every word before we print.

Is this appropriate? It sounds intense.

The letter is warm, comforting, and celebrates who they were – not how they died. It focuses on presence, love, and the life they lived. Families tell us it feels like hearing from them one more time.

Does this work for any relationship?

Yes. Mother, father, spouse, grandparent, sibling, child, close friend – the letter is written from their perspective, tailored to the relationship and the memories you share.

Will the letter actually sound like them?

We ask about their personality, how they spoke, and things they used to say. A grandmother who said "bless your heart" sounds different from a father who called everyone "kiddo." The more details you share, the more it sounds like them.

What if I'm not happy with the letter?

You see a full design proof before we print. Edit every word, regenerate the letter, or start over. If the finished piece doesn't meet your expectations, we'll make it right. 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Can I order this as a gift for someone else?

Yes. Many of our orders are gifts for someone who's grieving. Share what you know – even just a name and photo – and we'll create something meaningful. We can ship it directly to the recipient.
