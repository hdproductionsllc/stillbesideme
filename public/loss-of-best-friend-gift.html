Questions about loss of best friend gifts

How does a Letter From Heaven for loss of a best friend work?

You share memories about your best friend – their personality, things they said, how they made people feel. We write a first-person letter from their perspective, as if they're speaking directly to you. You approve every word before we print.

Is this appropriate for a friend, not a family member?

Absolutely. Friendship is its own kind of family. The grief of losing a best friend is real and deep, and it often goes unacknowledged by the rest of the world. This tribute honors the relationship as it deserves – fully and without qualification. You don't need to be related by blood for this to matter.

Can I send this to my friend's family?

Yes. Many people order a Letter From Heaven for the family of a friend who passed. You share your memories, and we write the letter from their loved one's perspective. It shows the family that this person mattered to people beyond the household – and it means more than you might expect.

What if I don't have many details to share?

Even a name, a photo, and one or two memories make a beautiful letter. The more you share, the more personal it gets, but we guide you through it. There's no wrong amount.

Can I edit the letter before it's printed?

Yes. You see a full design proof before anything goes to print. Edit any line, regenerate the letter, or start over. Nothing prints until you say it's right. 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Do you have memorial gifts for other relationships?

Yes. Letter From Heaven works for any relationship. Visit our Memorial Gifts page for all options.
